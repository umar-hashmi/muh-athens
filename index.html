KUL 39

ATHENS Course on Flexibility in Power System

One week ATHENS course organised by Dr Md Umar Hashmi and Prof Geert Deconinck on Flexibility in power systems, including lectures, labs, industry talks.
The course was delivered in Belgium from 16th to 20th March and was attended by 28 students from 8 universities.
It includes 10 industrial sessions and 2 invited lectures from MIT (USA) and VITO (Belgium).
